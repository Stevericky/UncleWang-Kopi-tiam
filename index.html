About Uncle Wang

Nestled in the heart of our neighborhood, Uncle Wang Kopi Tiam carries the warm tradition of the classic kopitiam. Here, the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the savory scents of traditional breakfast favorites. Uncle Wang, a friendly face you'll feel like you've known forever, welcomes every guest as family.


At Uncle Wang Kopi Tiam, we are passionate about local flavors and community. We believe in quality ingredients and recipes passed down through generations. Every dish from our menu tells a story – from the comforting Laksa broth to our handcrafted Roti Pandan. We hope to bring people together over a warm cup of kopi and a hearty meal. Selamat menikmati!
